solar_power Solar EPC Solutions

What is Solar EPC?

expand_more

Solar EPC stands for Engineering, Procurement, and Construction. It is a turnkey solution where SunPlus Power handles the complete lifecyle of a solar power project: engineering feasibility analysis, structural and electrical design, procurement of tier-1 components, site construction, net-metering validation, and utility grid commissioning.

What types of solar projects do you undertake?

expand_more

We design and construct high-performance solar infrastructure projects including commercial & industrial (C&I) rooftop systems, large-scale ground-mounted arrays, residential rooftop net-metering installations, solar micro-grids, and off-grid high-voltage battery storage systems.

Do you provide turnkey solar solutions?

expand_more

Yes, we deliver complete, end-to-end solar solutions. Our scopes cover shadow analysis, structure load calculation, discom approval documentation, equipment logistics, civil & electrical works, commissioning testing, and connection to state electricity grids.

How long does a solar installation take?

expand_more

Project timelines depend on scale: typical residential rooftop systems are installed and tested within 3 to 7 business days; commercial & industrial rooftops range from 2 to 6 weeks; while megawatt-scale ground-mounted utility arrays require 3 to 6 months of construction and grid integration planning.

Do you provide Operation & Maintenance (O&M) services?

expand_more

Yes, we provide standard and custom O&M packages. Our solar maintenance teams handle real-time SCADA performance monitoring, automated panel cleaning cycles, high-voltage electrical safety audits, thermal imaging analysis, and corrective component replacements.

How much can I save with solar?

expand_more

Implementing solar allows commercial and industrial customers to reduce electricity bills by up to 70% to 90%. The investment usually yields a payback period of 3 to 5 years, with the system continuing to generate free clean energy for its 25-year lifetime.

Do you assist with government subsidies and approvals?

expand_more

Yes, our regulatory division handles the complete compliance stack. We submit net-metering applications to state Discoms, organize local structure safety checks, clear electrical inspectorate (CEIG) requirements, and coordinate PM-Surya Ghar subsidy approvals for residential projects.

precision_manufacturing Manufacturing & GI Products

What products does SunPlus Power manufacture?

expand_more

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility produces Copper-Bonded Earthing Rods, Hot-Dip Galvanized (GI) Earthing Strips (sizes including 25*3mm, 25*6mm, and 50*6mm), Earthing Chemical Bags (earth enhancement material), heavy-duty PVC Earthing Inspection Chambers, rod clamps, and custom solar structural components.

Are your products tested for quality?

expand_more

Yes, all manufactured lots undergo strict in-house testing. Our products conform to national safety regulations, including IS 3043 code of practice for earthing, and undergo tests for coating thickness (minimum 100 microns copper coating for rods), electrical resistance, and mechanical tensile strength.

Can you manufacture customized products?

expand_more

Yes, we provide build-to-spec manufacturing. Our engineering facility can manufacture custom cross-sections of GI flats, specialized multi-pin lightning masts, terminal brackets, and customized structure mounting rails based on industrial drawing sheets provided by clients.

Do you supply products across India?

expand_more

Yes, SunPlus Power operates a national logistics network. We distribute our earthing products, chemical compounds, and structure rails PAN India, directly supplying major industrial projects, sub-stations, and warehouse zones.

Are your products suitable for industrial applications?

expand_more

Absolutely. Our earthing arrays and structural components are engineered to withstand massive fault currents and harsh weather. They are deployed in high-risk zones, including power transmission yards, corporate data centers, railway projects, oil refineries, and high-rise commercial structures.

Do you provide technical support for product selection?

expand_more

Yes, our engineering team assists with system design. We review local soil resistivity reports, calculate prospective fault currents, and recommend the exact volume of chemical compounds, rod dimensions, and conductor paths required for safety compliance.

What industries do you serve?

expand_more

We actively supply our products to solar developer firms, telecom infrastructure towers, electrical contractors, heavy engineering industrial plants, government distribution grids, and residential commercial real estate groups.

partner_exchange Dealers & Distributors

How can I become a SunPlus Power dealer or distributor?

expand_more

To start the process, navigate to our Connect tab and complete the Distributor Application. Alternatively, email your corporate deck and business registration documents to sales@sunpluspower.in. Our regional channel lead will get in touch with you.

What are the eligibility criteria?

expand_more

Eligible entities should possess active trade licenses in electrical or solar sectors, show sound financial credit, maintain warehouse storage space, and have an established sales network of contractors or retail stores within their assigned district.

What support do you provide to dealers?

expand_more

We support our partners with co-branded marketing materials, sample kits, regional technician training, wholesale price margins, and project bidding assistance. Additionally, inquiries originating from your territory are routed directly to you.

Is there a minimum order requirement?

expand_more

Yes, minimum transaction thresholds apply to authorize regional distributor tiers and unlock bulk pricing discounts. These volumes ensure consistent product availability across local electrical retail points.

Do you offer exclusive dealership opportunities?

expand_more

Yes, exclusive territorial rights are offered to distributors who achieve set quarterly target milestones and demonstrate deep coverage of local contractor networks.

How quickly are orders dispatched?

expand_more

Standard stock items (Earthing Chem Bags, standard copper-bonded rods, GI flats) are dispatched within 2 to 5 business days. Custom structure fabrication items require 7 to 15 business days depending on order size and custom design parameters.

How do I place bulk orders?

expand_more

Once registered and approved in our ERP, you can place bulk purchase orders through our portal, or send signed PO worksheets to purchase@sunpluspower.in for automated billing processing.

Do you provide marketing materials?

expand_more

Yes, we ship brand packages including hard-copy catalogs, display stands, sample rods, and banner designs. Co-branded digital PDF brochures can be configured by our marketing team for regional outreach.
